About

Yuan Liu

Yuan Liu is a Chinese photographer and visual artist born in China in 1995, currently based in Germany.

His work moves between street photography, architecture, portraiture and long-form visual projects, with a focus on urban atmosphere, spatial sensitivity and the quiet tension between people and place.

He studied fine art and photography in Düsseldorf and New York, learning from German artist Professor Michael Reisch and British street photographer Phil Penman.
