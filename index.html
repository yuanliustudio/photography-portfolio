About

Yuan Liu

Yuan Liu is a Chinese photographer and visual artist based in Germany. His work moves between street photography, architecture, portraiture and long-form visual projects. With a background in fine art, his practice is shaped by quiet observation, spatial sensitivity and an interest in the emotional distance between people, cities and images.
